ABOUT

Ajay Raju is theatre and screen experienced Indian actor who primarily works in Hindi Films, Web Series, TV Serials, Advertisements, Short Movies, Music Albums etc. he was born in Fulwariya a small village in Varanasi Uttar Pradesh. After completing his studies, he started doing private jobs there. In 2012 he came to Mumbai to study acting and did theater and doing acting in Bollywood.
